bio

Visualization tools are essential for deriving meaning from the deluge of data we are faced with today. In the Visualization Design Lab we create visualization systems that support exploratory, complex data analysis tasks for people and problems in the world. These systems allow researchers and scholars to learn about their data, reframe their problems, and disrupt their thinking.

We take a collaborative, problem-driven approach to research, relying on methodologies from computer science, design, and social science. Designing visualizations allows us to learn about the relationship of people with data and technology, as well as to learn about visualization design itself: techniques, guidelines, and approaches to rigorous design.
